Purpose of the roleAs a product compliance manager at Würth Korea your key responsibility is to make sure that all items that we sell to our customers are compliant with the local laws when it comes to registrations certifications labeling and all other processes demanded by Korean regulation for products to be allowed to be imported and sold Further you are responsible for ensuring compliance in a timely matter since our ambition is to grow our business by significantly increasing the number of products we are able to sell which requires ensuring product compliance firstThe product compliance manager will take on all tasks that relate to product compliance This currently entails especially all works related to the chemical compliance of technical chemical products1 Before import chemical products need to follow the processes required by KREACH and CCA Chemical Control Act This task will be executed with the support of partner organizations2 Further some products need to comply with additional laws such as the High pressure gas act KBPR Clean Air Conservation Act Petroleum and alternative fuel business act KC regulations and othersThe relevant laws are to be identified per product and it has to be ensured that the legal requirements are followed 3 For all chemical products safety data sheets SDS are provided with the help of a third party partner here following the KOSHA regulation the task is to register new SDS at the relevant authority and updating existing SDS in case of any relevant changes4 Finally all labels on chemical products need to comply with the demands resulting from Korean regulation and thus must be initially created with the support of a partner on design end and kept up to dateFor all these tasks the necessary execution and support mechanisms are available The responsibility of the product compliance manager is to ensure correct timely execution of all relevant compliance works In order to do so successfully collaboration with the international support team is crucial Thus English is the main language of communication in this roleMoving forward into the future the field of product compliance will further extend to ensuring compliance of for example power tools other electrical machines incl displays protective equipment etc
